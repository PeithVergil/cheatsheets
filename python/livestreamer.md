livestreamer
============


Installation
--------------------------------------------------
`pip install livestreamer`


Show supported sites
--------------------------------------------------
* Dailymotion
* Livestream
* Youtube
* Ustream
* Twitch


Playing a video
--------------------------------------------------
`livestreamer [http://www.twitch.tv/destiny] [high]`


Stream qualities/types
--------------------------------------------------
* source (best)
* high
* medium
* low
* mobile (worst)
* audio
